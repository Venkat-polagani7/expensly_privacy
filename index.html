Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Expensly ("we", "our", or "the App") — developed by Venkat Polagani — collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Expensly mobile application on Android.

By installing or using Expensly, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree with any part, please discontinue use of the application.

💬

SMS Access & Reading

Expensly requests the READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions to automatically detect and parse bank transaction SMS alerts (e.g., "₹500 debited from your account at Swiggy").

Important: Expensly only reads SMS messages that match known financial institution patterns. Personal messages, OTPs, and unrelated SMS content are never read, stored, or transmitted.

Specifically:

✓ Allowed Bank debit/credit alerts, transaction confirmations from registered financial institutions
✗ Never read Personal conversations, OTPs, promotional messages, or any non-financial SMS
✓ On-device SMS parsing happens entirely on your device — raw SMS content is never sent to our servers
☁️

Cloud Sync & Firebase

Expensly uses Google Firebase to sync your expense records across devices and enable backup. We use:

Firebase ServiceWhat It Does
Firebase Authentication Anonymous sign-in to link your data without requiring personal details
Cloud Firestore Stores your expense records (amount, merchant, date, category) for sync & backup
Anonymous Authentication: Expensly uses Firebase Anonymous Auth — you are never required to provide an email or password. A random anonymous UID is assigned automatically.

Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Your data is stored on Firebase servers located in the United States or other Google Cloud regions.

🔐

Biometric & App Lock

Expensly offers an optional biometric app lock (fingerprint / face unlock) to protect your financial data. This feature uses Android's BiometricPrompt API.

✓ On-device Biometric data (fingerprint/face) is processed entirely by the Android OS — Expensly never has access to your biometric data
✓ Secure Authentication credentials are stored in the Android Keystore hardware-backed secure element
Optional You can disable the app lock at any time from Settings
🔒

Local Storage & Encryption

All expense data stored on your device is encrypted with AES-256 using the Hive database with an encryption cipher. The encryption key is:

AES-256 Generated uniquely on first launch — never the same across devices
Keystore Stored in Android Keystore — hardware-backed, inaccessible to other apps
Local only The encryption key never leaves your device — even we cannot decrypt your local data

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.
