What Expensly Does

Expensly is an expense tracking app that reads your bank SMS alerts to automatically log your spending — so you don't have to enter anything manually. It shows you charts and summaries to help you understand where your money is going.

💬

SMS Messages

Expensly reads SMS messages from your bank or financial institution to detect transaction alerts (like "₹500 debited at Swiggy"). This is what powers the automatic expense tracking.

We only look at bank/payment messages. Personal texts, OTPs, and other SMS are completely ignored — and raw SMS content is never sent anywhere outside your phone.

All SMS reading happens entirely on your device.
☁️

Cloud Backup

Your expense data is backed up to the cloud so you don't lose it if you switch phones. We use Google Firebase for this — you're signed in automatically without needing an account or password.

Only your expense records (amount, merchant, date) are backed up — never your SMS messages.
🔒

Your Data is Safe

All data stored on your phone is encrypted. We don't sell your data, we don't show ads, and we don't share your information with anyone.

🗑️

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app (removes local data). To delete your cloud backup, just email us and we'll take care of it.
